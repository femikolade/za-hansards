<p><span class="col_195-196" refersTo="page_0116"/>In the second place, and linked to this, there is the fact that a far larger percentage of the teachers in the Coloured, the Asian and the Black education system are considerably less well-qualified and therefore also qualify for a considerably lower remuneration than in the case of the more advanced White educational system.
